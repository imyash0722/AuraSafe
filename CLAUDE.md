## Non-Negotiable Data Plan

Priority order for the scan flow: **barcodelookup.com first**, then CPDat ingredient hazard mapping.

### 1. barcodelookup.com API
- Resolve UPC/EAN barcode → product title, brand, category, ingredients, image
- Cache successful responses locally with timestamp
- If ingredients missing → fallback to Open Food Facts or manual entry

### 2. EPA Chemical and Products Database (CPDat)
- Map parsed ingredient strings to chemical hazard and exposure context
- Match strategy: exact → synonym → substring → fuzzy (difflib 0.75 cutoff)
- Generate confidence levels: HIGH / MEDIUM / LOW / unknown
- Bundled as `assets/cpdat_hazards.json` for offline use

### 3. Open Food Facts (secondary)
- Food product fallback with allergen data (`en:` tag mapping)
- Used when barcodelookup has no result or no ingredient text

## Risk Scoring Formula

```
Overall Score (1–10) =
    Health Score  × 0.50   (CPDat ingredient hazard)
  + Env Score     × 0.30   (disposal / environmental impact)
  + Personal Risk × 0.20   (allergy / condition conflict from MediQR)

Verdict:  1–3 = SAFE  |  4–6 = CAUTION  |  7–10 = DANGER
```

Confidence label is always shown. Unknown products score 5 (neutral) — never fabricated certainty.

## Android Camera Strategy

- **Android:** `ft.FilePicker` opens device camera or gallery → `zxingcpp` decodes barcode/QR from the image (pure Python, no native OpenCV)
- **Desktop:** Live OpenCV camera with `cv2.barcode.BarcodeDetector` + `QRCodeDetector`
- `services/camera_util.py` exports `is_android()`, `decode_barcode_zxing()`, `decode_qr_zxing()`

## Safety Guardrails (Non-Negotiable)

- No diagnosis claims — ever
- HIGH-severity symptoms (RED triage) always escalate to emergency services (112)
- All risk outputs show confidence labels and data source
- Unknown products flagged explicitly — score 5, verdict CAUTION
- Conservative outputs under uncertainty

## Presentation Key Points

- **Problem:** Invisible household toxins, medical data silos, triage panic with no guidance
- **Solution:** One offline app — scan a barcode to know its risk, carry your medical ID in a QR, get triage guidance without internet
- **Differentiator:** Unified chemical + personal health + environmental risk in one score; works fully offline
- **KPIs:** Time to triage escalation, classification accuracy, allergen conflict detection rate
- **Closing:** "AuraSafe operationalizes humanitarian safety in everyday decisions"
